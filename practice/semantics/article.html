Inoculation

Ever heard of post-modernism ? Well, this game embodies the philosphy of one of my favorite minds in the field. If you aren't familiar with his work I'd recommend watching/listening or reading about human nature in Chomsky vs. Foucault. Foucault's words, specifically in the debate, reflect what I had pondered for many years. As a student, I'd most often times innevitably daydream. I don't know why. It happened a lot in the United States. I'd completely recluse my presence into the mind and ingore everything and everyone. One day in 2012, I was in math class, and in my mind I played a game. Not unlike the games I would play while in Mexico...which were nightmares-at best. The fleeting sensation of power, intrigue and knowledge all boiled into abstractions. Inoculation is my response to all criticisms.

Pro Wrestling Text Adventure

This is a game about Pro-Wrestling. It's about the stories that happen every time a perfomer, sports entertainer, athelete steps into the ring. The game's main mechanics are about wrestling moves. The game's stories are the stories I wish were told in popular wrestling promotions. So if you like wrestling for its story telling or its incredible arsenal of moves, then you'd likely appreciate this second effort.
